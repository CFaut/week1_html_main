Ribbon:

The ribbon always looks very beautiful in routines because of the way it moves and adds to the gymnasts movements.

Ribbon is often called the hardest apparatus in rhythmic gymnastics - mostly due to the fact that it can knot very easily or get tangled

  • The ribbon is usually made of satin or a similar soft material.
  • It has to be minimum of 6m in length.
  • Can be any color - they are often multicolored and vibrant.
