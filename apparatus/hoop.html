Hoop:

The hoop is one of the most fun apparatus - routines often include rotation of the hoop around the body and throws in skillfull ways as well as leaping and turning within or while holding it.

However, similarly to the ball, if it is dropped in the competition or not caught after a throw, it can roll away, leading to deducted points with every step you take. Yikes!

  • Hoops are now mostly made of plastic so that the can bend a little instead of the traditional wood.
  • Measured according to gymnasts hip height.
  • They must weigh a minimum of 300 grams.
  • Hoops can be covered in customized tape to match the gymnasts leotard.
