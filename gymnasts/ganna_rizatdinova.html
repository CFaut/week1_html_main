Ganna Rizatdinova

Ganna Rizatdinova is a Ukranian rhythmic gymnast who won the bronze medal in the 2016 Olympic game, is a two-time world All-around medalist, and a two-time European All-around bronze medalist. She started competing in 2008 and was trained by her mother. She retired in 2017 to start her family.

She is on the list because of how determined and motivated she is. Her rouutines are performed with 100% of her effort and she has reaped the rewards as a result. She also has beautiful lines and symmetry in her movements.
