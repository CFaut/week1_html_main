Silvia Miteva

Silvia Miteva is a retired rhythmic gymnast from Bulgaria. She is the 2013 Grand Prix Final all-around bronze medalist. She was coached by her mother who was also a Bulgarian rhythmic gymnast and European champion. She started competing in 2001 and retired in 2013. she currently works as a gymnastics coach.

She makes the favorite list because of her 2012 Olympic routine which was beautiful.
