Son Yeon-Jae

Son Yeon-Jae is a retired rhythmic gymnast from South Korea. She is the 2014 Asian Games All-around champion and won the bronze medal at the same event in 2013, 2015 and 2016. She also competed at the Olympics games in 2012 and 2016.

She is a fan favorite because of her lively nad heartfelt performances. The crowd often claps along with her music because they're so captivated by her performance.
